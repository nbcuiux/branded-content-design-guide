What is Branded Content?

Branded Content

Branded content seeks to engender an emotional connection between the consumer and the brand with a greater focus on fostering a positive image than highlighting the actual product or service.

Content Marketing

Content marketing is more traditional and straightforward. The primary objectives are to highlight the product, its unique selling point, and grow its consumer base.

Brand Integration—One way to sneak in ads.

Brand integration is similar to product placement, but the product is central to the content’s plot line, giving the brand an opportunity to connect with its audience in a unique and engaging way.

Different Mediums

Music Videos

The video for Coldplay’s "Adventure of a Lifetime" features a major product placement for Beats By Dre. When Beats appeared in Coldplay’s video for 28 seconds, it generated a return of nearly $165,000 in the fourteen days after it was posted.

Viral Videos

In 2013, Dove released "Real Beauty Sketches", which had over 15 million downloads within a week. The viral video was a social experiment aimed to show women that they are more beautiful than they think by comparing their self-descriptions to those of a stranger. Since its opening campaign year, Dove sales have increased from $2.5 billion to $4 billion in 2014.

News Articles

In the Orange Is The New Black-sponsored article, "Women Inmates: Why the Male Model Doesn’t Work", writer Melanie Deziel reflects on how women’s incarceration experiences are vastly different from those of men in a system that is designed for men.

Print Magazine

Net-a-Porter built a digital shopping experience in a print magazine. Readers can shop their magazine through an app. Simply scanning a page displays the brand, price, and a buy button for any items offered on Net-a-Porter.

The magazine drove a 77% increase in advertising revenue. Net-a-Porter found that 46% of newsstand purchases were made by new customers.

Short Films

In 2016, after seeing a plunge in sales due to the E. coli outbreaks, Chipotle released a new animated short film, “A Love Story.” It aimed to highlight Chipotle’s commitment to using quality, fresh ingredients while criticizing rival fast food chains and their unsavory business practices.

Radio Stations

Pandora launched a campaign with PepsiCo's Propel water to target fitness enthusiasts during their workouts. Propel worked with fitness influencers to create three branded radio stations. Display and audio ads for Propel ran in each station. According to Millward Brown studies, the campaign increased ad awareness for Propel by 63% and purchase intent by 60%.

Podcasts

The Slack Variety Pack is a quirky magazine-style podcast about “work, life and everything in between” put out by the company that created the workplace communication tool, Slack. With the renaissance of podcasts, it is touted as the first branded entertainment podcast that aims to develop a deeper connection with its customers and further build its brand.

Feature Film

Warner Brothers’ film The Lego Movie boosted Lego sales by 13% to 28.6 billion Danish krone ($3.7 billion USD). A sequel to the original The Lego Movie is expected in 2019.
